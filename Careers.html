Our culture

At Apollo, we strive for excellence as hard as any other company, but it's the way we get there makes us different. We strive for kindness and transparency, and in the spirit of open-source, we're able to openly give each other feedback in a candid and constructive manner. As a result, we're constantly teaching and learning from each other, and therefore growing both professionally and personally.

Our Team

We love working with the most talented people we can find across the globe. Having a remote-first team is core to our company's culture, and we use the latest systems possible to ensure seamless collaboration between our remote teammates and those in HQ. In addition to using Slack for important conversations and Quip for documentation, we have high-quality videoconferencing and we record all meetings so they can be watched asynchronously for those in different timezones.
